African Fuel Co2 Emission (2020 -2022)

Description

Sustainable Development Goal 9 (SDG 9) , which aims to build resilient infrastructure, promote inclusive and sustainable industrialization, and foster innovation, this map supports the assessment of the africa continent Indicator 9.4.1 : CO₂ emissions per unit of value added from fuel combustion.

The visualization reveals an increasing trend in CO₂ emissions from fuel combustion across African regions between 2020 and 2022, indicating growing carbon intensity within energy and industrial systems.
